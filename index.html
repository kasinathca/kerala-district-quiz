About the Kerala District Quiz

Welcome to the Ultimate Kerala Geography Quiz! This interactive game is designed to help students, diverse travelers, and geography enthusiasts master the map of God's Own Country. Located on the southwestern coast of India, Kerala is known for its serene backwaters, lush green landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

Why Play This Map Game?

Whether you are preparing for a PSC exam or just curious about the location of **Wayanad's hills** or Alappuzha's backwaters, this quiz offers a fun way to learn. Instead of boring rote memorization, you get to interact with the map directly.

Master All 14 Districts

Our quiz covers every single district in the state: Thiruvananthapuram (Capital), Kollam, Pathanamthitta, Alappuzha, Kottayam, Idukki, Ernakulam, Thrissur, Palakkad, Malappuram, Kozhikode, Wayanad, Kannur, and Kasaragod.

Challenge your friends, beat your high score, and become a pro at Kerala's geography today!
